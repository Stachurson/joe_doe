2007 - Steve Jobs presents the first iPhone - the revolution on the market of mobile devices and smartphones has begun

Products

  • Iphone

    The first generation of the iPhone, called the 2G, was presented by Steve Jobs in 2007. Until 2013, a new model was presented every year, successively 3G, 3GS, 4, 4S, 5.

  • MacBook

    The MacBook is a brand of Macintosh laptop computers designed and marketed by Apple Inc. that use Apple's macOS operating system since 2006.

  • iMac

    iMac is a family of all-in-one Macintosh desktop computers designed and built by Apple Inc. It has been the primary part of Apple's consumer desktop offerings.

  • Apple Watch

    Apple Watch is a line of smartwatches produced by Apple Inc. It incorporates fitness tracking, health-oriented capabilities, and wireless telecommunication.
